I am Mercy (AKA) Lui with a passion for modeling. For the past 4 years I have been a freelance model and have done street styles, creative and designer shoots. To date I've worked with some amazing photographers, designers, make up artists, directors, producers, artists, stylists and brands. I am available for shoots in Mysore and Bangalore and can travel to other locations, I love being in front of the camera. I also have a presence as an influencer on social media. I’m 158 cm tall, have long hair and a medium skin tone. I love working with photographers and agencies that have a creative approach. I love experimenting with fashion as I believe that anything can look great on you as long as it’s worn in the right way. I put a 100% in all my work.
